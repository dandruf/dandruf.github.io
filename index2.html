New Computer Science Workshop Curriculum

Overview

Welcome to our new computer science workshop curriculum designed for 7-10th grade students and educators. Our curriculum focuses on teaching computer science fundamentals through project-based learning and interactive activities. Students will learn to program in various programming languages such as Python and JavaScript, understand data structures and algorithms, and develop problem-solving skills.

Curriculum Details

Our curriculum is divided into five modules:

  • Module 1: Introduction to Computer Science
  • Module 2: Programming Fundamentals
  • Module 3: Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Module 4: Web Development
  • Module 5: Final Project

Each module contains several lessons with interactive activities, programming challenges, and quizzes. Students will have access to our online learning platform where they can track their progress and receive feedback from our instructors.

Enrollment and Pricing

Enrollment is open for both individual students and groups. Our pricing is competitive and affordable for schools and homeschooling families. For more information, please contact us at info@computerscienceworkshop.com.
