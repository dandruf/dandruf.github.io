Welcome to the Computer Science Workshop!

We are excited to introduce our new computer science workshop curriculum, designed specifically for 7-10th grade students and educators. Our program covers a wide range of topics, including programming fundamentals, web development, algorithms, and data structures.

Our hands-on, project-based approach ensures that students gain practical experience while learning key concepts. With a strong emphasis on collaboration, play, audio interaction, and problem-solving, we aim to inspire the next generation of computer scientists and engineers.
